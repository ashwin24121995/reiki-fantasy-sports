# REIKI ENERGY - Complete Redesign Plan

## Design Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
- **Honest & Transparent**: No exaggerated claims, no fake promises
- **Educational Focus**: Clear messaging about skill-based learning
- **Modern & Professional**: Clean design with animations, gradients, images
- **User-Centric**: Easy to understand within first 3 seconds
- **Fully Dynamic**: Header/footer change based on login state

## Design System

### Colors
- **Primary Green**: #1B5E20 (Cricket field green)
- **Accent Gold**: #FFD700 (Trophy, highlights)
- **Royal Blue**: #1565C0 (Trust, professionalism)
- **Gradients**: Green to Blue, Gold to Orange
- **Dark Mode**: Deep blacks with subtle green tints
- **Light Mode**: Clean whites with soft shadows

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, clear, large
- **Body**: Readable, 16px minimum
- **Emphasis**: Gold color for important points

### Animations
- Fade in on scroll
- Slide up for cards
- Pulse for live match indicators
- Smooth transitions (0.3s ease)

### Images
- Cricket stadium backgrounds
- Player action shots
- Dashboard/UI screenshots
- Icons for features

### Spacing
- Generous white space
- Clear section separation
- Consistent padding/margins
